Beta Notice: Saarathi is currently in beta testing. You may encounter bugs or incomplete features. We appreciate your patience and feedback as we work to improve the app!

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What is Saarathi?
Saarathi is a beta todo application that helps you organize and manage your tasks efficiently. The name "Saarathi" means "charioteer" or "guide" in Sanskrit, reflecting our mission to guide you through your daily tasks.
Q: Is Saarathi free to use?
Yes! During the beta phase, Saarathi is completely free to use. We want to gather feedback and improve the app based on real user experiences.
Q: What features does Saarathi offer?
Saarathi offers task creation and management, priority settings, due dates, and a clean, user-friendly interface. More features are being added regularly as we develop the app.
Q: Is my data safe?
Yes, we take data security seriously. Please review our Privacy Policy to learn more about how we collect, store, and protect your information.
Q: How can I report bugs or suggest features?
We welcome your feedback! Please contact us using the email address in the Contact section below. Your input helps us make Saarathi better.
Q: Will my data be preserved when the app exits beta?
We will make every effort to preserve your data during the transition from beta to the full release. However, as this is beta software, we recommend keeping backups of important information.
