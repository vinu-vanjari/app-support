At Saarathi, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use our beta todo application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information to provide and improve our services:

Personal Information

  • Account Information: Email address, username, and password (encrypted)
  • Profile Information: Optional display name and preferences

Usage Information

  • Task Data: Todo items, descriptions, due dates, priorities, and categories you create
  • App Usage: Features you use, frequency of use, and interaction patterns
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, browser type, and IP address
  • Log Data: Error logs, crash reports, and performance metrics

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Service Delivery: To provide, maintain, and improve Saarathi's functionality
  • Synchronization: To sync your tasks across multiple devices
  • Personalization: To customize your experience and remember your preferences
  • Communication: To send important updates, security alerts, and respond to your inquiries
  • Analytics: To understand usage patterns and improve app performance
  • Security: To protect against fraud, abuse, and security threats
  • Beta Testing: To identify bugs, gather feedback, and enhance features during the beta phase

3. Data Storage and Security

We implement robust security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption: Your data is encrypted in transit using SSL/TLS protocols and at rest using industry-standard encryption
  • Secure Servers: Data is stored on secure servers with restricted access and regular security audits
  • Password Protection: Passwords are hashed and salted using secure algorithms
  • Regular Backups: We maintain regular backups to prevent data loss
  • Access Controls: Only authorized personnel have access to user data, and only when necessary

Data Retention: We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You can request deletion of your account and data at any time.

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • With Your Consent: When you explicitly authorize us to share information
  • Service Providers: With trusted third-party service providers who help us operate the app (e.g., hosting, analytics), under strict confidentiality agreements
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or to protect our legal rights
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, with notice to users
  • Aggregated Data: We may share anonymized, aggregated data that cannot identify individual users

Important: We will never share your individual task content or personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

5. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate personal information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Export: Request a portable copy of your data in a common format
  • Opt-Out: Unsubscribe from marketing emails (if applicable)
  • Restriction: Request restriction of processing under certain circumstances
  • Objection: Object to processing of your personal data

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information provided below.

6. Cookies and Tracking

We may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for app functionality and authentication
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how you use the app (can be disabled)
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your settings and preferences

You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings.

7. Children's Privacy

Saarathi is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will promptly delete it.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of significant changes by:

  • Posting the updated policy on this page with a new "Last Updated" date
  • Sending an email notification to your registered email address
  • Displaying a prominent notice within the app

Your continued use of Saarathi after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or your personal information, please contact us:

Email: privacy@saarathi.example.com

Support Email: support@saarathi.example.com

We will respond to your inquiry within 30 days.

10. Beta Software Notice

Please note that Saarathi is currently in beta testing. During this phase:

  • Features and data handling practices may change
  • We may collect additional diagnostic information to improve the app
  • We recommend not storing highly sensitive information in the app
  • We will notify users of any significant privacy-related changes
